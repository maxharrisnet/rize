## Mission

Technology has the power to change lives, expand reach, and open doors. But that power only lands when people feel confident using it — when it fits the way they already work and the goals they are already chasing.

Rize Skill Training exists to make that happen. We bring practical, accessible technology education to individuals and organizations across Vancouver and the Greater Vancouver area — with a focus on the communities, entrepreneurs, and creatives who have the most to gain and have historically had the least support in getting there.

We are technology guides, not technology salespeople. We help people find the tools that actually fit their work, build the skills to use them well, and free up more time for the impact only they can make.

## Funded partnerships

Provincial and federal programs are actively funding digital skills and AI upskilling training for specific communities right now. We help identify what applies, handle the program design and facilitation, and deliver a workshop or course your community genuinely benefits from — often at little or no cost to you.

We work with community organizations, nonprofits, Indigenous-led groups, Black community organizations, libraries, employment centers, and social service agencies across Vancouver and the surrounding area.

## How it works

### Step 1: Reach out
Tell us who you are, who your community is, and what you are hoping to achieve.

### Step 2: We design together
We identify the right program, confirm the format, and where applicable, identify funding sources that can cover the cost.

### Step 3: We show up and deliver
We handle the curriculum, the facilitation, and the materials.

### Step 4: You keep the momentum
Participants leave with real skills and resources, and organizations can continue with follow-up support or next-step programs.
