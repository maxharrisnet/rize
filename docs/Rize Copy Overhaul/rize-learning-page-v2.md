## How learning works

### Workshops
Single sessions or short series running 90 minutes to half a day. Practical and focused, designed to deliver immediately useful skills. Available through community partnerships and on a public schedule.

### Courses
Multi-session programs running 4 to 6 hours total, designed to build something functional by the end — a workflow, a digital presence, a grant-writing system, or a marketing strategy. Delivered through organizational partnerships.

### Delivery options
- Partnership delivery — we come to your organization, your space, your community.
- Public schedule — open sessions anyone can register for.
- Custom engagements — tailored programs for specific groups, industries, or cohorts.

### Funded partnerships
Provincial and federal funding is available for many of these programs right now, particularly for digital skills and AI upskilling for specific communities. We can help identify what applies and work through the process alongside you.

## Community

### Community programs
Technology should feel useful, understandable, and within reach. This section is for people and organizations that want more confidence, more safety, and more capacity in everyday digital life.

### Digital Confidence
Everyday technology skills for people who want to feel more in control online. Most technology training assumes people already know the basics. This workshop starts from the beginning and builds real confidence with the tools people use every day — at a pace that works for everyone in the room.

**Who this is for:** Seniors ready to get more comfortable with smartphones, email, and video calling; newcomers navigating digital services and online accounts; people returning to the workforce; community members ready to engage more fully with technology in daily life.

**What you will walk away with:** Confidence using email, smartphones, browsers, and video calling tools; the ability to manage online accounts safely and independently; a foundation for continued learning at your own pace.

### Stay Safe Online
Protect yourself from scams, phishing, and the new wave of AI-powered threats. Online scams have become more convincing and harder to spot — especially as AI tools are increasingly used to create them. This workshop gives people the knowledge and practical tools to recognize threats, protect their accounts, and make confident decisions online.

**Who this is for:** Seniors who want to protect themselves from phone and online scams; social media users concerned about privacy and account security; community members who want to recognize and respond to suspicious messages; anyone who wants to feel more confident and in control of their digital safety.

**What you will walk away with:** The ability to identify scams and phishing attempts with confidence; practical steps to secure accounts and protect personal information; an understanding of how AI is being used in online threats — and how to respond.

### Organizations That Move People
For nonprofits, charities, community-led groups, and social service organizations that want to do more with technology — reaching more people, operating more efficiently, and making their mission go further.

**Who this course is for:** Nonprofit and charity staff at any level; community organization leaders and program managers; Indigenous-led and Black-led community organizations; social service agencies and settlement organizations; religious and cultural organizations with community programs.

**Topics covered:** Automating internal workflows; fundraising tools and digital donor engagement; AI-assisted grant writing and reporting; volunteer management systems and onboarding workflows; community engagement; research and impact-reporting methods.

**What you will walk away with:** At least one functioning workflow that saves your team meaningful time each week; a grant writing process supported by AI tools and reusable templates; a community engagement strategy your team can manage independently; confidence using technology as a force multiplier for your mission.

## Career

### Career programs
This section stays focused and learning-centered. It is for people who want to build a stronger professional presence, understand how AI is changing work, and move forward with confidence.

### Future-Proof Your Career
Use AI and modern tools to get hired, stay competitive, and build a professional presence that works for you. The job market is changing fast. AI is reshaping what employers expect, what skills matter, and how people find and land opportunities. This workshop gives job seekers and career changers a practical, grounded strategy for staying ahead.

**Who this is for:** Job seekers modernizing their approach; career changers building a new professional identity; recent graduates; newcomers establishing their professional presence; anyone using AI to work smarter in a job search.

**What you will walk away with:** A clear understanding of how AI is changing the job market and a strategy to stay ahead; a stronger LinkedIn/resume/online presence; AI-generated cover letters and application materials; a personalized learning path; tools and prompts to keep using.

### Career-ready custom sessions
For employment centers, workforce partners, youth programs, settlement organizations, and other groups that want modular sessions tailored to their audience and goals.

## Creative

### Creative programs
Creative work deserves tools that support visibility, sustainability, and momentum. This section is for artists and creative professionals who want to build stronger systems around their work.

### Creative Work, Amplified
For artists, musicians, filmmakers, designers, writers, and other creative professionals ready to grow their audience, fund their work, and build a sustainable practice using modern tools.

**Who this course is for:** Musicians releasing albums, EPs, or building a touring presence; filmmakers and video producers developing and distributing projects; visual artists and designers building an online presence and client base; writers and journalists developing platforms and monetizing their work; multidisciplinary creatives managing multiple projects and revenue streams.

**Topics covered:** Building a creative digital presence; marketing your work; monetization strategies; AI-assisted grant writing for arts funding; project launch workflows; managing your creative business.

**What you will walk away with:** A launch-ready digital presence; a monetization strategy tailored to your creative practice; a completed grant application or reusable grant writing template; a project launch playbook you can apply to every future release.
