# Rize Skill Training — Updated Site Outline

## Primary structure

### 1. Home
- Lead with a clear promise.
- Route visitors into the core paths.
- Use a stronger CTA such as **Get Started** or **Start Here**.
- Include a short explanation of who the organization serves and what kinds of outcomes it creates.

### 2. Community
- Primary learning and services page.
- Digital Confidence and Stay Safe Online as the main Series.
- Internal nonprofit / community transformation as the partner-facing services layer.
- Websites and internal tooling for community organizations.
- Strong funding and partnership language.

### 3. Career
- One focused Series page.
- Job readiness, AI and work, modern professional presence.
- Keep this learning-centered.
- Avoid building a separate services stack here.

### 4. Entrepreneur
- Practical business support page.
- Websites.
- Automation and internal tooling.
- Business workflow improvement.
- Optional learning Series if needed.

### 5. Approach
- Formerly How It Works.
- Explain partnership relationships.
- Explain steps from first conversation to delivery.
- Explain tailoring and customization.
- Fold funded partnerships into this page.
- Explain what people and organizations walk away with.

### 6. About
- Founder story.
- Values.
- Local and community roots.
- General contact info and form at the bottom.
- Add a booking CTA that leads to a separate booking page.

### 7. Book
- Separate booking or inquiry page for active next steps.
- For people ready to schedule a discovery call or partnership conversation.
- This should feel action-oriented and simple.

### Individual session pages
- Later, after the core Series are defined.

### Public schedule

## Better CTA language

Instead of a generic contact button, use:
- **Get Started**
- **Start Here**
- **Book a Discovery Call**
- **Explore the Path**
- **Talk About a Partnership**

For the homepage, **Get Started** or **Start Here** is the cleanest top-level CTA.

For the bottom of About, use **Book a Discovery Call** or **Start a Conversation**.

---

## Recommended page logic

The site should now work like this:

- **Home** introduces the organization and routes people.
- **Community / Career / Entrepreneur** show the core offers.
- **Approach** explains how partnerships work.
- **About** builds trust and contains general contact details.
- **Book** handles actual scheduling and next steps.

That keeps the site organized by audience and outcome, while keeping the action pathway clear.

---

## Strongest launch focus

The launch should stay centered on:
- Community digital confidence and safety.
- Community organizational transformation.
- Career upskilling.
- Entrepreneur support.

Creative can wait.
