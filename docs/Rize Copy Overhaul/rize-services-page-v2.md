## How services work

Every service engagement starts with a discovery call. We learn about your organization, your goals, and your current setup, then give you a clear recommendation, a realistic scope, and a practical path forward.

### Typical flow
- Discovery call.
- Scope and recommendation.
- Build and setup.
- Handoff and training.
- Optional ongoing support.

## Websites

A strong website helps people understand who you are, trust what you do, and take the next step. Rize builds websites that support real-world goals like community outreach, registrations, donations, bookings, program communication, and local visibility.

### Website service areas
- Community and nonprofit websites.
- Events and programming sites.
- Creative and service-based websites.

### What these sites can support
- Public presence and trust-building.
- Program and event communication.
- Registration, donations, bookings, or inquiries.
- Mobile-friendly, accessible content management your team can handle after launch.

## Internal systems

Internal systems are the behind-the-scenes structures that make an organization run better day to day. Rize helps organizations reduce repetitive work, improve follow-up, and build better operational flow with practical tools and lightweight automation.

### Internal transformation areas
- Google Workspace setup and cleanup.
- Communication workflows.
- Donation and CRM workflows.
- Reporting and follow-up systems.
- Automation for repetitive admin.

## Grant support

Rize also supports organizations in identifying and applying for funding related to digital skills training, AI upskilling, and technology-enabled capacity building. In many cases, this support is what helps a training or systems project become possible in the first place.

### Grant support can include
- Funding source identification.
- Application support and co-writing.
- Program design language for submissions.
- Reporting templates and follow-up materials.

## Who this is for

- Nonprofits and charities.
- Community organizations and community-led groups.
- Social service, settlement, and employment organizations.
- Libraries and public-serving community spaces.
- Indigenous-led groups and nations.
- Black-led community organizations.
- Creative or entrepreneurial teams that need public-facing websites or workflow support.

## Closing section

Rize can support the full arc of the work: training your people, building the supporting systems, and helping your organization use those tools well over time. Some partners start with a workshop, some start with a website or workflow problem, and some need both from the beginning.
