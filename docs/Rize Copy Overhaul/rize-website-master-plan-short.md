# Rize Skill Training — Website Master Plan

This document is the current source of truth for the website structure and page-direction decisions for weallrize.ca.

## Canonical sitemap
- Home
- Learning
- Services
- Partnerships
- About

## Page roles

### Home
Purpose: Introduce Rize clearly and route visitors to the right path.

### Learning
Purpose: Present Rize's education offer through the approved CCC structure — Community, Career, Creative.

### Services
Purpose: Present implementation support clearly and separately from learning.

### Partnerships
Purpose: Explain the partnership model, funded-fit logic, and relationship process.

### About
Purpose: Build trust, communicate story and values, and provide contact pathways.
