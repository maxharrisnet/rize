# Rize Skill Training — Booking Page Intake Spec V1

This file locks in the current booking/intake form direction for the website.

## Page purpose
The booking page is the main action page for direct inquiries and partnership conversations. Its job is to collect enough structured information to route the lead, prepare a useful first response, and support automated estimate generation.

## Primary CTA
Book a Discovery Call.

## Secondary CTA
Talk About a Partnership.

## Form structure

### Section 1: Contact basics
- Name.
- Organization name.
- Email.
- Phone number, optional.
- Website, optional.

### Section 2: Who you are
Dropdown:
- Community organization.
- Nonprofit.
- Social service agency.
- Library.
- School.
- Business.
- Entrepreneur.
- Individual.
- Other.

### Section 3: What you need
Checkboxes:
- Digital Confidence.
- Stay Safe Online.
- Future-Proof Your Career.
- AI and Modern Workflows.
- Entrepreneurs in Motion.
- Organizations That Move People.
- Creative Work, Amplified.
- Custom workshop.
- Custom course.
- Website.
- Internal systems.
- Grant support.
- Automation.
- Not sure yet.

### Section 4: Partnership and funding
- Are you interested in a funded partnership?
- Do you already have funding?
- Would you like help finding funding?
- Which communities do you serve?
- Are there accessibility, language, or delivery considerations?

### Section 5: Scope
- Desired format: workshop, short series, course, service project, not sure.
- Delivery mode: in-person, online, hybrid.
- Preferred timeline.
- Estimated group size.
- Budget range.
- Would you like follow-up support?

### Section 6: Open context
- Tell us a little about what you are hoping to build or solve.

## Backend mapping
The form should feed these core fields into automation:
- Inquiry type.
- Audience type.
- Offer selection.
- Delivery format.
- Funding status.
- Timeline urgency.
- Budget band.
- Scope complexity.
- Follow-up need.

## Lock-in decision
This is good enough for the current launch stage. Keep the form structured, practical, and easy to expand later without changing the core intake model.
