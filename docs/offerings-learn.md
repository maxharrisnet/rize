## What Learn is

Workshops and courses that close the **technology gap** — the distance between what today's tools
make possible and what people have time to figure out. We cut through the noise, focus on the skills
people actually need, and leave them more capable and confident. Delivered across Vancouver and the
surrounding area. Accommodating all skill levels.

## Formats

- **Workshops** — single sessions or short series, 90 minutes to half a day. Practical and focused;
  immediately useful skills.
- **Courses** — multi-session programs, 4–6 hours total, designed to build something functional by the
  end (a workflow, a digital presence, a grant-writing system). Delivered in partnership with an
  organization that brings the audience and space; we build alongside the internal team.

## Delivery options

- **Partnership delivery** — we come to your organization, your space, your community.
- **Public schedule** — open sessions anyone can register for.
- **Custom engagements** — tailored programs for specific groups, industries, or cohorts.

Many programs are grant-fundable (see [`grant-strategy.md`](grant-strategy.md)).

## Workshops

### Digital Confidence — _Track 1_
- **Tagline:** Everyday technology skills for people who want to feel more in control online.
- **Summary:** Most technology training assumes people already know the basics. This workshop starts
  from the beginning and builds real confidence with the tools people use every day — at a pace that
  works for everyone in the room.
- **Who it's for:** Seniors (smartphones, email, video calling); newcomers navigating digital services
  and online accounts; people returning to the workforce; community members engaging more fully with
  everyday technology.
- **Walk away with:** Confidence using email, smartphones, browsers, and video calling; ability to
  manage online accounts safely and independently; a foundation for continued learning at your pace.
- **Skill level:** All levels, including complete beginners.
- **Format:** 90 minutes to half-day. In-person or online. Single session or multi-week series.

### Stay Safe Online — _Track 1_
- **Tagline:** Protect yourself from scams, phishing, and the new wave of AI-powered threats.
- **Summary:** Online scams have become more convincing and harder to spot — especially as AI tools
  are used to create them. This workshop gives people the knowledge and practical tools to recognize
  threats, protect their accounts, and make confident decisions online.
- **Who it's for:** Seniors protecting themselves from phone/online scams; social media users concerned
  about privacy and account security; community members recognizing suspicious messages; anyone wanting
  more control over their digital safety.
- **Walk away with:** Ability to identify scams and phishing with confidence; practical steps to secure
  accounts and protect personal information; an understanding of how AI is used in online threats — and
  how to respond.
- **Skill level:** All levels.
- **Format:** 90 minutes to half-day. In-person or online.

### Future-Proof Your Career — _Track 2_
- **Tagline:** Use AI and modern tools to get hired, stay competitive, and build a professional presence
  that works for you.
- **Summary:** The job market is changing fast. AI is reshaping what employers expect, what skills
  matter, and how people find and land opportunities. This workshop gives job seekers and career
  changers a practical, grounded strategy for staying ahead.
- **Who it's for:** Job seekers modernizing their approach; career changers building a new professional
  identity; recent graduates; newcomers establishing their professional presence; anyone using AI to
  work smarter in a job search.
- **Walk away with:** A clear understanding of how AI is changing the job market and a strategy to stay
  ahead; a stronger LinkedIn/resume/online presence; AI-generated cover letters and application
  materials; a personalized learning path; tools and prompts to keep using.
- **Skill level:** All levels. Bring a laptop or smartphone if you have one.
- **Format:** Half-day. In-person or online.

### AI and Modern Workflows — _Track 2_
- **Tagline:** Spend more time on the work only you can do. Let technology handle the rest.
- **Summary:** Entrepreneurs, community organizations, and creatives are stretched thin. Meaningful work
  gets crowded out by admin, coordination, and repetitive tasks. This workshop shows how to use AI and
  modern tools to reclaim that capacity — expanding what you can do, who you reach, and your impact.
- **Who it's for:** Entrepreneurs and small business owners; nonprofit/community staff reaching more
  people with the same team; creatives who want to create more and administer less; anyone curious
  about putting AI to practical use.
- **Walk away with:** A practical read of the AI landscape and which tools are worth your time;
  hands-on with Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, NotebookLM; an intro to workflow automation (n8n, Slack);
  prompt engineering basics; a clear picture of where technology expands your reach most.
- **Skill level:** All levels. Curiosity is the only prerequisite.
- **Format:** Half-day. In-person or online. Can be tailored to specific industries or organization types.

## Courses

### Entrepreneurs in Motion
- **For:** People building their own business, freelance practice, or side project — ready to use
  technology to build faster, reach further, and work smarter.
- **Who it's for:** Early-stage founders and solo entrepreneurs; freelancers and independent service
  providers; side-project builders professionalizing; small business owners modernizing operations.
- **Topics:** Building your digital presence (website, social, local search); automating internal
  workflows with AI and tools like n8n and Slack; automating sales/marketing (email sequences, lead
  capture, follow-up); AI-assisted business strategy and research; identifying and researching new
  opportunities with AI.
- **Walk away with:** A working digital presence tailored to your business; at least one automated
  workflow saving time weekly; a clear AI strategy for day-to-day operations; tools, templates, and
  prompts to keep building on.

### Organizations That Move People
- **For:** Nonprofits, charities, community-led groups, and social service organizations that want to
  reach more people, operate more efficiently, and make their mission go further.
- **Who it's for:** Nonprofit/charity staff at any level; community org leaders and program managers;
  Indigenous-led and Black-led organizations; social service and settlement agencies; religious and
  cultural organizations with community programs.
- **Topics:** Automating internal workflows (tasks, communications, scheduling); fundraising tools and
  digital donor engagement; AI-assisted grant writing and reporting; volunteer management and
  onboarding; community engagement (social, email, events); research/impact-reporting methods.
- **Walk away with:** At least one functioning time-saving workflow; a grant-writing process supported
  by AI and reusable templates; a community engagement strategy your team can manage independently;
  confidence using technology as a force multiplier for your mission.

### Creative Work, Amplified
- **For:** Artists, musicians, filmmakers, designers, writers, and other creative professionals ready to
  grow their audience, fund their work, and build a sustainable practice using modern tools.
- **Who it's for:** Musicians (releases, touring); filmmakers/video producers; visual artists and
  designers; writers and journalists; multidisciplinary creatives managing multiple projects/revenue.
- **Topics:** Building a creative digital presence (website, portfolio, social, streaming); marketing
  (audience building, email lists, content strategy); monetization (digital sales, licensing,
  crowdfunding, membership); AI-assisted grant writing for arts funding; project launch workflows;
  managing the creative business (invoicing, scheduling, client comms).
- **Walk away with:** A launch-ready digital presence; a monetization strategy for your practice; a
  completed grant application or reusable template; a project-launch playbook for every future release.
