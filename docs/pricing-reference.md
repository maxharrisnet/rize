## Website packages

| Package | Price | For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Starter** | from **$750** | New organizations, solo founders, and anyone launching their first real site. |
| **Standard** *(featured)* | from **$1,500** | Established groups running programs, events, or a steady flow of clients. |
| **Growth** | from **$2,500** | Organizations and businesses ready to invest in a system, not just a site. |

**Starter** — A clean, professional presence that gets you found and trusted, fast.
- Up to 5 pages, mobile-first and accessible (WCAG 2.1)
- Contact form and email newsletter signup
- Local SEO and Google Business Profile setup
- Built on WordPress so your team can edit content

**Standard** — Room to grow: events, a blog or booking, and the integrations that save time.
- Up to 10 pages
- Blog, events, or online booking section
- Email marketing and CRM integration
- Donation or payment setup — Canada Helps, Stripe, PayPal
- Enhanced local and AI search visibility

**Growth** — A tailored site with automations and training so your team fully owns it.
- Up to 20 pages with custom content types
- Donation systems, memberships, or e-commerce
- Workflow automations that reduce manual work
- Team training and documentation
- Priority support through launch
