## What Systems is

We build **systems that make organizations run better** — not one-off sites. We deliver
**transformation, not tasks**: eliminate the annoying repetitive work, put new capabilities in reach,
and turn saved time into more reach and impact. Built for organizations and businesses across Vancouver
and the surrounding area. Everything is **owned by the client** — their site, accounts, and workflows —
not dependent on us indefinitely.

Page: `/systems` (formerly `/services`). Nav label: **SYSTEMS**. Pairs with **LEARN**.

## How the offering is structured

- **Transformation packages lead** — 2–3 named, outcome-oriented bundles headline the page.
- **Service categories stay below** — detail for buyers who already know what they want.
- **Website pricing tiers** (Starter/Standard/Growth) are attached to the website category and carry
  listed "from $" prices. **Packages themselves have no listed price** — each starts with a free
  discovery call.

## Transformation packages

### 1. Own Your Website
- **Promise:** A professional site your team updates without calling a developer.
- **For:** Organizations and businesses that want a real web presence — and full control of it.
- **Includes:** WordPress site designed around your goals/community/voice; accessible (WCAG 2.1),
  mobile-first, built for local and AI search; hands-on team training (editing pages, posting updates,
  adding events); full ownership and documentation (your site, accounts, keys).
- **Vignette:** A neighborhood arts nonprofit used to email their web person — and wait — for every
  event listing. Now the program manager posts events in minutes and the site keeps up with the work.

### 2. Get Your Time Back
- **Promise:** The repetitive work that eats your week, set up to run itself.
- **For:** Teams losing hours to follow-up emails, triple-posted updates, and copying information
  between tools.
- **Includes:** A working session to map where the hours go; automation for repeat work (follow-ups,
  reminders, posting, data entry); integrations connecting Slack, HubSpot, Google Workspace, and the
  tools you already use; documentation and training so the team owns and adjusts every workflow.
- **Vignette:** A coordinator's Monday used to go to copying weekend registrations, posting the same
  update three times, and answering the same five emails. Now registrations file themselves, one update
  posts everywhere, the website answers the questions — and Monday belongs to the participants.

### 3. Reach More People
- **Promise:** Bigger reach with the same team.
- **For:** Organizations and businesses ready to grow their audience without growing their workload.
- **Includes:** A website chatbot answering common questions 24/7 in your voice; email and social
  outreach that sounds like you and goes out on schedule; local search and AI visibility (Google, maps,
  AI answers); feedback/signup/follow-up systems that listen and respond without the busywork.
- **Vignette:** A family-run studio answered booking questions at 10pm and posted socially "when there
  was time." Now the chatbot answers instantly, classes are promoted weekly automatically, and new
  students keep finding them.

## How it works (every engagement)
1. **Discovery call** — 20 minutes, free, no commitment.
2. **Proposal** — clear scope, clear price, plain language.
3. **Build and deliver** — we do the work and keep you informed throughout.
4. **Handoff and training** — we walk your team through everything so you can own it.

## Service categories (detail)

### For Organizations
- **Community and Nonprofit Websites** — story, programs, easy donate/register/get-involved, WordPress
  self-editing. Donation integration (Canada Helps, Stripe, PayPal incl. recurring); event/program
  pages; volunteer management and forms; newsletter/donor tools; WCAG 2.1, mobile-first.
- **Events and Programming Sites** — sell tickets, build audience, drive attendance. Ticket integration
  (Eventbrite, Ticket Tailor); program/registration pages; sponsor recognition/galleries; email + social.
- **Grant Writing Assistance** — identify provincial/federal funding for digital skills & AI upskilling;
  application support and co-writing; program-design documentation; reporting templates. _Often what
  makes a funded partnership possible — see [`grant-strategy.md`](grant-strategy.md)._

### For Businesses and Entrepreneurs
- **Booking and Appointment Sites** — online booking (Calendly, Acuity, Square); service/pricing pages;
  intake forms and automated confirmations; team pages and availability.
- **Local Business Sites** — Google Maps/Business Profile setup; local + AI search optimization;
  hours/location/click-to-call; menu, service listings, galleries.
- **Creative Portfolio Sites** — gallery/portfolio; music and video embeds (Spotify, SoundCloud, Vimeo,
  YouTube); press kit / media downloads; booking and contact.
- **E-Commerce and Online Stores** — store setup (Shopify, WooCommerce, Squarespace); product/inventory
  management the team owns; secure checkout/payments/shipping; digital products, subscriptions, pickup.

### Integrations and Automations
Connect the tools you already use so information flows and work happens without manual triggers. Tools:
n8n, Zapier, Claude AI, Slack, and more. Built reliable, maintainable, and yours to own.

- **What we connect:** CRM (HubSpot, Mailchimp) to site/email/lead capture; booking/scheduling to
  calendar and client comms; donation/payment platforms to site and donor follow-up; social + email
  marketing to your content workflow; internal tools (Slack, Teams) to task/project management; AI tools
  (Claude, ChatGPT) to content/research; Google Workspace / Microsoft 365 to daily operations.
- **In practice:** contact form → CRM entry + welcome email + Slack notice; new event → auto social post
  + email; grant draft generated from a template and routed for review; donation → thank-you email +
  donor DB update + follow-up task.

### Growth and Outreach
- **Digital Marketing Strategy** — a realistic plan across social, email, content, and search, built
  around what your team can maintain.
- **Local Search and AI Visibility** — show up on Google, maps, and AI-generated answers; profile, local
  presence, and content optimization.
- **Community Engagement** — social management, email list building, event promotion, and feedback
  systems that actually listen.
