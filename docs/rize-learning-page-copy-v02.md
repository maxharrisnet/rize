## Intro

## Learning that meets people where they are.

Rize Skill Training offers practical learning experiences for people building confidence with everyday technology, strengthening their work and career skills, growing a business, or supporting a creative practice. Every program is designed around real use, real goals, and the actual comfort level people bring into the room.

We do not expect every group to start from the same place. We work with organizations and internal teams to shape delivery around the audience, the timeline, the learning level, and the community context so participants can engage in a way that feels useful, accessible, and grounded.

---

## How programs work

## Workshops and courses, shaped around the people in the room.

**Workshops**

Single sessions or short series running from 90 minutes to half a day. Practical, focused, and designed to give people skills they can use right away.

**Courses**

Multi-session learning experiences for groups ready to go deeper. Courses are designed to help participants build confidence over time and leave with a clearer way to use technology in their work or everyday life. Selected courses can also be delivered through video.

**Delivery options**

- Partnership delivery — we come to your organization, your space, and your community
- Public sessions — selected workshops offered on an open schedule

**How partnership delivery works**

Every partnership starts with a conversation about who the program is for, what they need, and what success looks like in your context. We work with your internal team to shape the format, pacing, accessibility, cultural safety, and delivery approach so the program fits the people it is meant to serve.

That can include adjusting the timeline, adapting examples and activities, meeting people at their current learning level, and building a program that works for your space, your staff, and your community. The goal is not just to deliver content. The goal is to create a learning experience people can actually use.

**Funded partnerships**

Many organizations want to offer this kind of learning but need support with funding, planning, or program design. Rize works with community organizations, nonprofits, Indigenous-led groups, Black-led organizations, libraries, employment programs, and other trusted partners to bring workshops and courses into the spaces where people already gather.

Where relevant, we can help identify funding pathways and shape a program package that fits both community need and available support.
